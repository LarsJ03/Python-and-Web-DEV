About This Project

This Blackjack game is a school project designed to showcase web development and programming skills. It is purely educational and is not intended for serious gambling or real money use.

Learn Blackjack

Blackjack, also known as 21, is one of the most popular casino games in the world. The aim is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Each player starts with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end. Players can 'Hit' to draw more cards or 'Stand' to stop. The dealer must hit until their cards total 17 or higher.
